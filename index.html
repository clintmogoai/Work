Recent Projects

A selection of case studies that exhibit my process as a user experience designer

Act Now - Social cause platform

Act Now! is a social activist platform that allows users to keep up to date and be informed on political and social events happening around them.

HouseMate - Mobile app design

HouseMate is an app for people in share houses, with the goal of simplifying household processes and reducing interpersonal conflict between housemates.

Non-profit WRD

A team web redesign challenge focused on streamlining the registration and donation processes for the tradies for the bushfire rebuild website.

Recomondo - Mobile app design

Travel app to assist solo travellers to fully immerse themselves in the culture of a destination by taking part in authentic and real travel experiences.
